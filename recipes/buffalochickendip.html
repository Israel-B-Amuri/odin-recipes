Bafalo Chicken Dip

Description

We are going to make a buffalo chicken dip. I will try by all means to keep this recipe as simple as possible. I suppose we share the same love for cooking and hope you have all the required ingredients for making this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Creame Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hot Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over medium until heated through for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in the cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well blended and warm for 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in 1/2 of the cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top.
  3. Cover and cook on low until the dip is hot and bubbly for about 35 minutes.
  4. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.
