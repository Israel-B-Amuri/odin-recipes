Guacamole Recipe

Description

I am going to direct you in the teps of making guacamole dip. I hope that you'll love the end result of this recipe.

Before getting started with this making the dip, I'd like to give you a little background of guacamole dip. This dip is an avocado based dip or spread of mexican origin. It's made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. It also contains onions and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados -peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper(optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Server immidiately, or Cover and refrigerate for an hour for improved flavour
